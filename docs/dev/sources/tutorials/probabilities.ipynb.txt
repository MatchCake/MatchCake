    "# Probabilities\n",
    "\n",
    "MatchCake can efficiently compute computational-basis probabilities\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "p(y) = \\left|\\langle y | V | \\psi_\\text{prod}\\rangle\\right|^2, \\qquad y \\in \\{0,1\\}^n,\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "for any matchgate circuit $V$ applied to any qubit product state\n",
    "$|\\psi_\\text{prod}\\rangle = \\bigotimes_{k=0}^{n-1}(a_k|0\\rangle + b_k|1\\rangle)$.\n",
    "This works for computational-basis inputs as well as arbitrary product states\n",
    "such as $|+\\rangle^{\\otimes n}$.\n",
    "\n",
    "Both the full distribution over all $2^n$ outcomes and the marginal distribution\n",
    "on a subset of wires are available through the standard `qml.probs` measurement.\n",
    "Each probability reduces to a Pfaffian of the evolved Majorana covariance matrix, so\n",
    "the per-outcome cost is polynomial in $n$ rather than exponential."
   ]

    "## Single-outcome probability\n",
    "\n",
    "When only one outcome is needed, the probability of a basis state $|y\\rangle$ is the\n",
    "expectation value of the projector $|y\\rangle\\langle y|$. Measuring\n",
    "`qml.expval(qml.Projector(y, wires))` evaluates a single Pfaffian directly, avoiding\n",
    "the full $2^n$ enumeration."
   ]
  },
  {
